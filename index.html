Kybella

Kybella is an FDA-approved injectable treatment that dissolves fat cells under the chin. During the treatment, a series of injections are administered into the chin area. The active ingredient in Kybella is deoxycholic acid, a molecule that breaks down fat cells. Over the following weeks, the fat cells are gradually eliminated by the body’s natural processes.

CoolSculpting

SCoolSculpting is a non-invasive treatment that uses freezing technology to eliminate fat cells. During the treatment, a special applicator is placed under the chin, which freezes the fat cells. Over the following weeks, the fat cells are eliminated by the body’s natural processes.

Ultherapy

Ultherapy is a non-invasive treatment that uses ultrasound technology to tighten and lift the skin under the chin. During the treatment, a special applicator is placed on the skin, which emits ultrasound waves. The waves penetrate deep into the skin, stimulating collagen production and tightening the skin.

FAQs About Double Chin Removal

If you’re considering double chin removal in Pensacola, you probably have some questions. Here are some of the most common questions we hear:

Who is a Good Candidate for Double Chin Removal?

Good candidates for double chin removal are generally in good health and have excess fat under their chin that doesn’t respond to diet and exercise. It’s important to have realistic expectations about the results of the treatment.

How Long Do the Results Last?

The results of double chin removal treatments can vary, but they are generally long-lasting. Once the fat cells are eliminated, they don’t come back. However, it’s important to maintain a healthy lifestyle to prevent the formation of new fat cells.

Are Double Chin Removal Treatments Painful?

Most double chin removal treatments are minimally invasive and don’t require anesthesia. Some patients may experience mild discomfort during the treatment, but this can be managed with over-the-counter pain medications.
